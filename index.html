The Arena

Step into the chaotic, relentless battlefield where survival is the only goal. Waves of twisted creatures swarm from all sides, each deadlier than the last. As the lone hero, you must harness powerful, evolving abilities to fend off the hordes. Collect mysterious relics, unlock devastating weapons, and discover hidden power-ups as you push your limits in the ever-tightening arena. The only question is—how long can you survive the onslaught before the darkness claims you?
