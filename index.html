Step into the chaotic, relentless battlefield where survival is the only goal. Waves of twisted creatures swarm from all sides, each deadlier than the last. Collect mysterious relics, unlock devastating weapons, and discover hidden power-ups as you push your limits in the ever-tightening arena.
